Questions?

Answers to your most asked questions

General Questions

When and where is the wedding?

Paul and Gwendolyn are getting married on December 26, 2025 in Accra, Ghana. The ceremony will be held at Kingdom Hall at 2:00 PM, followed by reception at Idrowyte Event Center at 5:00 PM.

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by November 26, 2025. This deadline helps us finalize our guest count and catering arrangements. If you have a special circumstance, please reach out to us directly.

What should I wear?

We invite you to wear cocktail or formal attire. The dress code is elegant and semiformal. Think dresses, suits, or traditional formal wear. We'd love to see you celebrate in something that makes you feel amazing!

Logistics

Is transportation provided?

Yes! We provide shuttle service from selected hotels to both the ceremony and reception venues. When you RSVP, indicate if you need transportation, and we'll arrange pickup times for you.

Where should I stay?

We've arranged special discounted rates at several hotels near the venues. Check our Venue page for hotel recommendations. Mention "Paulyn Wedding" when booking to receive your discount!

What's the parking situation?

Ample parking is available at both venues. Valet service will be provided at the reception venue for your convenience, so you won't need to worry about parking.

Dietary & Accessibility

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! Please indicate any dietary restrictions when you RSVP. We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, and other dietary needs. We want everyone to enjoy the celebration!

Is the venue accessible?

Both venues are wheelchair accessible. If you have specific accessibility needs, please reach out to us at rsvp@paulynwedding.com, and we'll ensure everything is arranged for your comfort.

Can I bring children?

Children are welcome at our celebration! Please include them in your RSVP. We recommend discussing any childcare or seating preferences when you respond.

Photos & Sharing

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We ask that you refrain from taking photos during the ceremony so everyone can fully experience the moment. Feel free to take photos during the reception and cocktail hour!

How do I share photos with you?

Tag us with #Paulyn on Instagram and social media! We'll be collecting all photos with this hashtag to create a beautiful shared album of memories from our special day.

Transportation & Parking

Shuttle service will be provided from selected hotels to both venues. Please indicate your need for transportation when you RSVP. Ample parking and valet service will be available at the reception.

Getting to Accra

Kotoka International Airport (ACC) is the main airport serving Accra. Direct flights are available from many international destinations. For getting around Accra, we recommend using registered taxi services or ride-sharing apps like Uber and Bolt.
